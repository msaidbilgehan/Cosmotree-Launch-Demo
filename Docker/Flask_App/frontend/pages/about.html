Our Platform

In collaboration with HPE Ezmeral, we've developed a state-of-the-art platform that streamlines various IT processes. This platform is designed to:

  • Collect HPE software logs from cluster nodes.
  • Facilitate FQDN (Fully Qualified Domain Name) setups.
  • Efficiently remove HPE software from clusters.
